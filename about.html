About Us

History

Blue Trail, founded in 2023, is a Houston-based Salesforce consulting firm operating as a sole proprietorship. Our primary focus is to deliver tailored Salesforce solutions for small businesses, helping them thrive in a competitive market. We are committed to providing our clients with exceptional service, guiding them through the complexities of the Salesforce ecosystem with our extensive expertise.

Team

Located in Houston, Texas, Blue Trail was established by a Senior Salesforce Administrator with over five years of experience and two prestigious certifications. Our founder has assembled a dedicated team of professionals, including three Junior Salesforce Administrators, who share a passion for empowering small businesses with the power of Salesforce. As a close-knit team, we are able to maintain a personal touch and a deep understanding of each client's unique needs.

Services

Our core services include Salesforce Implementation, Customization, Integration, Training, and Support. By providing a comprehensive suite of solutions, Blue Trail ensures that our small business clients can leverage the full potential of the Salesforce platform to streamline their operations, improve customer relationships, and drive growth.

Mission

At Blue Trail, our mission is to become the go-to Salesforce consulting firm for small businesses, recognized for our dedication to customer success, deep expertise, and commitment to building lasting relationships. We strive to help our clients navigate the ever-evolving Salesforce landscape, delivering tailored solutions that drive measurable results and position them for long-term success.
